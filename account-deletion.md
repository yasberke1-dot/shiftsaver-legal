# Account Deletion Request

**ShiftSaver** is developed and operated by **Berke Yas** (ABN: 39 221 377 601).

## How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your ShiftSaver account and all associated data, please follow one of the methods below:

### Option 1: Contact Your Administrator
Ask your organisation's ShiftSaver administrator to delete your account from the admin panel.

### Option 2: Email Us Directly
Send an email to **berke@ankadev.com.au** with the subject line:

**"Account Deletion Request - ShiftSaver"**

Please include:
- Your full name
- Your registered email address
- Your organisation name

We will process your request within **7 business days**.

## What Data Gets Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:
- Your profile information (name, phone, email)
- Your shift history
- Your progress notes
- Your timesheets
- Your GPS check-in/out records

## Important Note

Some data may be retained for legal and compliance purposes as required by NDIS regulations.
