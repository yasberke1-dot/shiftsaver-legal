# Privacy Policy

**ShiftSaver** ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Ongoing Care ABN [YOUR ABN].

Last updated: April 2026

## Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ShiftSaver:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates during shift check-in and check-out
- **Work Data:** Shift details, progress notes, incident reports, timesheets
- **Device Information:** Push notification tokens for sending alerts
- **Signatures:** Digital signatures collected from NDIS participants

## How We Use Your Information

- To manage and track support worker shifts
- To verify worker attendance via GPS
- To generate timesheets and invoices
- To send push notifications about shift updates
- To comply with NDIS documentation requirements

## Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on AWS infrastructure in Singapore. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is only shared with:
- Your employer (NDIS provider organisation)
- Supabase (our database provider)

## NDIS Compliance

ShiftSaver is designed to assist NDIS providers with shift management. All participant data is handled in accordance with the NDIS Privacy Safeguard requirements.

## Camera Permission

ShiftSaver requests camera access solely for capturing participant signatures during shift completion.

## Location Permission

Location data is only collected during shift check-in and check-out to verify worker attendance.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time
